This privacy policy describes how this personal blog collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information Collection

This is a personal blog. I collect minimal information:

- **Comments**: When you leave a comment using Giscus, your comment is stored in a GitHub Discussion linked to this website's repository. Giscus uses your GitHub account information to authenticate you. Please refer to [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for details on how GitHub handles your data.

- **Server Logs**: Standard web server logs may collect your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information is used for security and performance monitoring only.

## Third-Party Services

This website uses:

- **Giscus** for comments, which is powered by GitHub Discussions. By commenting, you agree to GitHub's terms of service and privacy policy.

## Cookies
